Saturday, July 4th, 2020
3:00 - 10:00 PM
Berkley Riverfront

What can I bring with me to RiverFest?

YES

• Empty Water Bottles (NO glass)
• Lawn Chairs
• Blankets
• Sunscreen
• Bug Spray
• Jackets & Ponchos
• Unbrellas
• Diaper Bags
• Small Purses
• Strollers/wagons

NO

• Food or drink of any kind (including water)
• Alcohol
• Drug paraphernalia
• Glass bottles or containers
• Coolers
• Backpacks
• Large purses and bags
• Pets (Service animals are permitted)
• Weapons (including but not limited to firearms and knives)
• Fireworks of any kind (including sparklers)
• Papering, leafletting or material distribution of any kind

KC RiverFest and Berkley Riverfront Park reserves the right to modify this list without notice.
KC RiverFest is a rain or shine event and will do everything in its power to conduct its fireworks display in a safe and timely manner on July 4th. The safety of our patrons is our number one priority and KC RiverFest, Port KC and the City of Kansas City, Missouri will make an appropriate determination for the execution of the fireworks display based on the weather forecast, weather sources and forecast conditions. There are no refunds or exchanges in the event that fireworks are not displayed.
