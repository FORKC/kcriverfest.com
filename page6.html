FAQs

What can I bring with me to RiverFest?

YES:
* Empty water bottles (NO GLASS)
* Lawn Chairs
* Blankets
* Sunscreen
* Bug Spray
* Jackets & Ponchos
* Personal Umbrellas (no patio umbrellas)
* Diaper Bags
* Small Purses
* Strollers/wagons

NO:
* Backpacks
* Large Purses and bags
* Tents
* Coolers
* Food or drink of any kind (including water)
* Alcohol
* Drug Paraphernalia
* Weapons (including but not limited to firearms and knives)
* Fireworks of any kind (including sparklers)
* Pets (service animals permitted)
* Glass bottles or containers
* Papering, leafletting, or material distribution of any kind

     KCRiverFest and Berkley Riverfront reserve the right to modify this list without notice.

Are credit/debit cards accepted?

Admission is CASH ONLY. There will be ATMs available at the park entrance and scattered throughout the park for your convinience.

Some vendors may accept credit/debt cards.

When do the fireworks start?

Fireworks generally start between 10:00 and 11:00 PM and as soon as it is dark enough.

KCRiverFest is a rain or shine event and will do everything in its power to conduct the fireworks display in a safe and timely manner on July 4th. Safety is our number one priority and KCRiverFest, FORKC, PortKC, and the city of Kansas City, MO will make an appropriate determination for the execution of the fireworks display based on weather conditions.

 How much is admission and is there re-entry?

Admission is $5.00 CASH ONLY for adults. Kids 12 & under are free.
There is NO RE-ENTRY.

There are no refunds or exchanges in situations where the event is cancelled or the fireworks are not displayed.
