Volunteer Positions

Setup/Teardown

 

* Assists with setting up or taking down chairs, tables, signage, activities, and merchandise.
* Helps with general park clean-up and maintenance.

Front Gate

 

* Take cash for ticket sales and provide accurate change.
* Answer basic questions about schedule of events and locations of activities.

Kid's Area

 

* Setup games and activities
* Assist with arts & crafts

Makers Area

 

* Assist vendors throughout the day
* General maintenance of the area

Green Room

 

* Assist bands with staging areas
* Maintain stock of food & beverages
* Maintain stage viewing area

Merchandise Tent

 

* Collect cash and provide accurate change
* Store unsold items and return to storage
