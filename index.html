About the Fest

KC RiverFest is the premier Independence Day celebration in the region, located at beautiful Berkley Riverfront from 3 - 10 p.m. on July 4th.




Entertainment

Celebrate the 4th of July with the BEST fireworks show in the region, plus two stages of music, including a performance from the USAF Band of Mid-America's Jazz Ensemble! 




Activities

Featuring family-friendly entertainment and activities, an abundance of food vendors and refreshing beverages, KC RiverFest is the ultimate Independence Day celebration.
